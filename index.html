About FEUROOSEVELT

FEU Roosevelt, formerly known as Roosevelt College, is a private institution located in Rodriguez, Marikina, and Cainta, and is part of the Far Eastern University (FEU) group. It offers a variety of academic programs, including business, information technology, and hospitality management courses. Known for its commitment to quality education, the institution emphasizes the development of students skills and values to prepare them for the professional world. FEU Roosevelt also promotes a nurturing environment with modern facilities and a focus on holistic student development, creating opportunities for academic, social, and personal growth.
